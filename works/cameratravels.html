Camera Travels

Single channel video with sound, dur:4 min 43 sec, 2007

A video that discusses the ability of images to exercise social criticism given the power that the producers of such visual (or other) discourse posses. Such a criticism can only be effective if issues of subjectivity, authorship and the mechanics of such productions are taken into consideration.


Its theme evolves around the projections of self images by two disparate groups that live in Cyprus, the artists and the third world immigrants who share a nomadic way of living, brought about for different reasons.
