Sissyphus

Single channel video with sound, dur:9 min 53 sec, 2018

A free depiction of the punishment of Sisyphus who having cheated death was condemned to an eternity of rolling a rock uphill, then watching it roll back down again. The music score by Galina Dimova follows the global stocks index chart for the years 2002 until 2017, using the graph as notation. All shots taken in cement quarries and abandoned unfinished housing estates in Cyprus
