A Selection of Works by Yiannos Economou

1995 - 2025

The core material of most of the artworks comes from images and sounds from the outside world. This applies more clearly to the works of the last two and half decades. Further artistic manipulation, such as editing and mixing, serves to convey artistic perceptions and ideas. But the works never stray from reality.


The return to reality is a pragmatic turn. Simulacra, virtual reality, hyperreality, AI, can define our way of perceiving the world up to a point. The current conditions we live in demand a more solid approach. At the bottom of it is climate change. You can argue that all experience passes through media filtering, but the atmospheric heat will damage your body, extreme weather conditions will damage your property. When you faint because of a sun stroke it can not be a simulation. And there is no safety anywhere on the planet.


The works have a traditional structure. Narrative, even in a loose form, is always present. Accessibility to all social strata is adhered to.
