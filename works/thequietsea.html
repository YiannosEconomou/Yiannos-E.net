One Day At the Quietes Sea

Photography into Video

A series of photographs made for the Artists Book exhibition "One Of A Kind" curated by Ruth Keshishian in Nicosia. The book was sold so the photographs - mostly shot with a medium format 6X4.5 Mamiya Camera - are put here in a video with just the basics of the story.
