In Between

35 mm Analogue Photography, 1993 - 1995

In Between is a series of photographs which started with the post-graduate final show in Canterbury and was concluded with the film 2000 Miles and the solo show Short Instances.

The photographs explore the theme of transition, both physical and metaphorical, capturing moments that exist in the liminal spaces of life. The images reflect a journey through various landscapes and urban settings, highlighting the fleeting nature of time and the ephemeral quality of human experience.
